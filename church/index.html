WELCOME TO QODESH FAMILY CHURCH STL NORTH

We shall not be few!

Our church is about empowering the young people to change the world for Jesus. Jesus is calling each of us to join him in the work of saving others. We stand for that everyday. Since 2015 under the leadership of Pastor Titus Prince Codjoe, The QFC St. Louis North has won, prayed for, trained, and shepherded over 1,000 young people to know, get close to do the work of God and propagate the Gospel of Jesus Christ. The QFC St. Louis North is also a non charismatic ministry and has opened doors for unique relationships and ministry opportunities using choirs, orchestras, jazz bands, worship teams and music education.
